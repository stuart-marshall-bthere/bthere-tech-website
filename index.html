Helping seniors live in their own homes

Living in one's own home is great

Your parents want to live in their own home. It's where they feel comfortable. You want to help them live at home as long as possible. You also want to know they are safe.

bThere is bringing modern technology and services to help people age in place.

bThere Sense

The bThere Sense lets you know that your older loved ones are active and keeping to their habits. The bThere Sense is a small, innovative device that sits under the senior's TV. It sees the senior show up in the morning, go about their day, and when they head off to bed. It sends this information to cloud services for smart analytics.

The bThere service sends notifications to you if it detects unusual and worrisome patterns, like Mom being two hours overdue in the morning. You can also check in and see that all is well.

The senior doesn't have to do or learn anything. It's all automatic. Setting it up is easy: plug in the device and putting it on the wifi.

Your senior's data is kept secure and private. You can purchase the device alone and get activity notifications for free. Or you can sign up for a photo subscription to see photos from your loved one's living room.
